1. Introduction & Who We Are

2. Information We Collect

We collect information in the following ways:

  • Information you provide: name, email address, phone number, company, role, and anything else you submit through forms, demo requests, or messages.
  • Information collected automatically: IP address, browser type and version, operating system, referring URL, pages viewed, and timestamps.
  • Cookies and similar technologies: see Section 6.
  • Communications: messages you send us via SMS, email, WhatsApp, or phone.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to demo requests and provide our services
  • Communicate with you about your account, our product, and support
  • Send marketing emails (only when you've opted in — you can unsubscribe at any time)
  • Measure and improve our website, products, and marketing
  • Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security incidents
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Legal Basis for Processing

We process personal information based on your consent, on the necessity of fulfilling a contract with you, on our legitimate business interests (e.g., improving our services, securing our infrastructure), or to comply with applicable law.

5. Server Logs

When you visit our website, our servers automatically log basic technical data (IP address, browser type, pages visited, timestamps). This data is used for security, debugging, and analytics, and is typically retained for up to 7 days.

6. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies (including local storage) to operate the website, analyze usage, and serve relevant advertising. Strictly necessary cookies are set without consent. Analytics and marketing cookies are only set after you opt in via our consent banner.

You can withdraw consent at any time through the cookie banner or your browser settings.

7. Demo Booking (Scheduling Tool)

When you book a demo, we use a third-party scheduling tool. We collect your name, email address, phone number (if provided), and meeting preferences in order to confirm and run the meeting.

8. CRM & Marketing Automation (HighLevel)

We use HighLevel (HighLevel Inc., USA) to manage prospect and customer communication, automate marketing and sales workflows, and store contact and conversation history. Data processed includes contact details, communication logs, lead status, and usage data.

9. Meta (Facebook & Instagram) Pixel & Ads

With your consent, we use the Meta Pixel and related services (Conversions API, Custom Audiences, retargeting) provided by Meta Platforms, Inc. This may transmit information about your activity on our website to Meta and link it with your Meta profile, in order to measure ad performance and serve relevant ads on Facebook and Instagram.

More info: facebook.com/privacy/policy

10. Google Services (Ads, Analytics, Tag Manager, YouTube, Fonts)

With your consent, we use the following Google services (Google LLC):

  • Google Ads & Conversion Tracking — measure ad performance and run retargeting
  • Google Analytics 4 — measure website usage (IP anonymization enabled)
  • Google Tag Manager — manage tracking tags (does not set cookies on its own)
  • YouTube — embedded videos in privacy-enhanced mode
  • Google Fonts — serve web fonts

More info: policies.google.com/privacy

11. TikTok Pixel & TikTok Ads

With your consent, we use the TikTok Pixel (TikTok Inc.) to measure and optimize advertising performance on TikTok.

12. LinkedIn Insight Tag

With your consent, we use the LinkedIn Insight Tag (LinkedIn Corporation) to measure ad performance and run retargeting on LinkedIn.

13. Email Marketing

We send marketing emails using a confirmed opt-in process. We process your email address, name (if provided), and engagement data (opens, clicks) to measure and improve campaign performance. You can unsubscribe at any time via the link in every email we send.

14. SMS & Business Texting

Where you've provided your mobile number and opted in, we communicate with you via SMS (text message). Data processed includes your name, phone number, message content, and delivery metadata. Messages are sent through licensed A2P SMS carriers in compliance with applicable U.S. regulations (including 10DLC registration where required). Standard message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out at any time, or HELP for assistance.

15. WhatsApp Business API (International)

For international contacts, where you've opted in, we may communicate with you via the WhatsApp Business API (operated by Meta). Data transmitted includes your name, phone number, message content, and communication metadata.

16. Social Media Profiles

We maintain profiles on platforms including Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, TikTok, and YouTube. The respective platform operators are responsible for processing data on their platforms. If you interact with us on those platforms, the operators may collect data about you. For details, please refer to each platform's own privacy policy.

17. Service Providers

We use carefully selected service providers to operate our business, including for hosting, CRM, email, payment processing, analytics, and advertising. These providers process personal data on our behalf under appropriate contractual safeguards.

18. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as needed for the purposes described in this policy, or as required by applicable law (e.g., tax and accounting record retention). Once no longer needed, we delete or anonymize the data.

19. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share data with service providers who help us run our business (Section 17), with advertising and analytics partners (only when you've opted in), and where required by law or to protect our rights.

20. Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect your personal information. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, but we work continuously to protect the data we hold.

21. Your Choices

You can opt out of marketing emails using the unsubscribe link in any of our messages. You can withdraw cookie consent through our consent banner. To request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information, contact us at info-mail@syncraos.io.

22. California Privacy Rights (CCPA / CPRA)

If you are a California resident, you have the following rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act, as amended by the CPRA:

  • The right to know what personal information we collect, use, disclose, and sell or share
  • The right to request deletion of your personal information
  • The right to correct inaccurate personal information
  • The right to opt out of the sale or sharing of your personal information
  • The right to limit the use of sensitive personal information
  • The right to non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights

We do not sell personal information for monetary value. We may share certain information with advertising partners for cross-context behavioral advertising; you can opt out via our cookie banner. To exercise any of these rights, email us at info-mail@syncraos.io.

23. Other U.S. State Privacy Rights

Residents of states with comprehensive privacy laws (including but not limited to Virginia, Colorado, Connecticut, Utah, and Texas) may have similar rights to access, correct, delete, and opt out of certain processing. Contact us at info-mail@syncraos.io to exercise these rights.

24. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 16, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us so we can delete it.

25. International Visitors

We are based in the United States. If you access our services from outside the U.S., your information will be transferred to and processed in the U.S. and other countries where our service providers operate. Where applicable, we rely on Standard Contractual Clauses or other valid transfer mechanisms.

26. Do Not Track

Our website does not currently respond to "Do Not Track" browser signals. We honor cookie preferences set through our consent banner.

27. Changes to This Policy

We update this policy when our services or applicable laws change. The current version is always available on this page.

28. Contact Us

Questions about this policy or how we handle your data? Email us at info-mail@syncraos.io.
